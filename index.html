About conference

The sentence "What I cannot create, I do not understand" attributed to physicist Richard Feynman, summarizes the spirit of DivIDE, that wants to push the ambition of modern-day structural biology to the limit of what is currently possible. The focus of this conference will be the structural understanding of the spindle. We aim to bring together scientists working towards ..., quantitative imaging of the spindle, theoretical models, and developing new tools that aim to provide new insights into the structural understanding of the spindle.
